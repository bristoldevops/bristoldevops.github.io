### An introduction to the tools we use to ensure the absolute maximum of up time.

As well as our own hosting offerings, our customers often require their own hosting platforms. These providers, especially cloud hosting providers, often bundle different monitoring systems with their packages. Wherever possible we'll add the monitoring systems provided to our alerting systems so we can help ensure your site stays up without causing additional server load to your systems.
